- What is the difference between ***var*** and ***let***?
The difference between Var & Let is that Var you can redeclare and reasign variables while with let you can reasign but not redeclare variables.

- What is the difference between **var** and ***const***?
While Var can reasign and redeclare variables, const on the other hand prevents a variable from ever being redeclared and reasigned.

- What is the difference between **let** and **const**?
The difference between the two is that with let you can reasign variables while with const you can't.

- What is holsting?
Holsting is the way of explaining how javascript compiler works.
